About me

Hi, I am Tiya. I'm an entrepreneur and a wellness & empowerment coach based in Sydney, Australia. I have studied Positive Psychology at the University of North Carolina and Computer Engineering at the National University of Singapore.

I run a community organisation called Empowered Beauties With Brains. I believe that every woman has beauty, brains and power within. She just needs to be aware of it and be in touch with her inner self.

The intention of the organisation is to help, support, motivate, inspire, uplift and empower women. We implement various programs, workshops, events, training and coaching sessions for the benefit of women in Australia, Singapore and India.
